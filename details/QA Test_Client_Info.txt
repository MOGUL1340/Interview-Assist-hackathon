Manual QA Engineer (Senior)

Employment type: Full-time
We are seeking a highly skilled Senior Manual QA Engineer to lead quality assurance activities for enterprise-level immersive applications (VR/AR/Web) running on browser, tablet and mobile devices. 
The ideal candidate is detail-oriented, process-driven, and experienced in testing complex digital ecosystems with multiple integrations.
Key Responsibilities:
Develop and execute comprehensive test plans, checklists, and regression suites.
Ensure cross-platform functionality (Web, iOS, Android, Meta Quest).
Validate complex UI/UX flows, API responses, and data integrity.
Coordinate test efforts for releases, manage bug triage, and support UAT.
Mentor junior QA team members and ensure testing best practices.
Work closely with development and product teams to ensure release readiness.
Requirements:
4+ years of experience in manual QA for web or mobile apps.
Strong knowledge of QA methodologies, SDLC, and bug life cycle.
Experience testing CMS-based systems and digital content workflows.
Familiarity with tools like Jira, Confluence, TestRail, and Postman.
Analytical mindset, excellent communication skills, and attention to detail.
Upper-Intermediate or higher English.
Nice to have:
Experience with XR/VR/AR projects or 3D visualization systems.
Familiarity with Charles Proxy or other proxy-like tools
Basic understanding of automation testing or CI/CD pipelines.
